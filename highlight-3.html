The Narrative

d'Alba is a Korean skincare brand positioned within the premium beauty market, recognized for luxury ingredients, minimal aesthetics, and a refined tone of voice.
This project reimagines one of its most recognizable products, the UV Waterfull Essence Sunscreen, through a clearer balance of luxury and function, repositioning the product as an essential part of everyday protection.

Concept

The redesign is built on the idea that what you protect defines what you value. Rather than treating sunscreen as a seasonal or secondary skincare step, the rebrand positions sun protection as an intentional daily act of care.

Inspired by Italian luxury values of craftsmanship, restraint, and timelessness, the new identity shifts emphasis toward clarity, trust, and hierarchy, allowing SPF efficacy to lead while preserving the elegance of the brand.

Protection is no longer a feature.
Protection becomes the luxury.

A strategic rebrand focused on repositioning d'Alba sunscreen as a premium essential in everyday protection. The process translated a clear brand concept into a cohesive identity system across packaging, retail, and campaign touchpoints.

The Execution

Big Idea

d'Alba's sunscreen line is reimagined around a simple truth: what you protect defines what you value.

In a fast-changing cosmetics market, this redesign restores hierarchy by placing protection first and aesthetics second. The experience feels secure, structured, and intentional, mirroring the product's purpose.

Protection is not an added benefit; it is the luxury itself.
